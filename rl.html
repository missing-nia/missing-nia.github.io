RougeLite Indev

This project is a RougeLite I am working on with some associates, with me currently being the sole programmer on the team. Although the game is still early in development, I have done extensive work so far on various gameplay systems and mechanics, such as level generation, pathfinding, enemy AI, buffs/debuffs, and more.

Assets are largely placeholders or work in progress as the project is still very early in development.

Dungeon Generation Demonstration

Showcase of my dungeon generation for several random seeds. Dungeon is generated iteratively using an array to manage occupied room coordinates to prevent overlaps and other errors. Currently generates a spawn room, an exit, a relic room, and several randomized filler rooms. After room generation, room connections are determined and doors are added, with walls filling in unused connection spaces on the tilemaps (shown in the second demo video).

Enemy AI

Currently there are three enemy types, with one being a simple pathfinder to the player, and the other two (wolf and bird) being more complex. The wolf AI (white circle) will roam aimlessly unless the player enters its aggro range where it will begin performing charged attacks. Bird (blue circle) tracks the player and attempts to find a line-of-sight before firing. Enemy pathfinding is handled using the Theta* pathfinding algorithm (modification of A*)

Buffs and Debuffs

Buffs (and debuffs) are handled on update ticks, with buffs being applied, reapplied, and removed at fixed intervals via event triggers. Player buffs show up in the bottom left corner, with the player able to see a description for each using a keybind. Debuffs appear over the healthbar of enemies via a mini icon.

Buffs showcased are Adrenaline (speed boost for a few seconds when entering a room), Boiling Blood (stacking damage up on kill), and Poison (ticking damage over time).
